Interesting facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of iguana suborder
  • Changing skin colour is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting facts about Panda

  • Panda's have great camouflage for their environment
  • Panda's have an extended wrist bone tht they use like a thumb to help them grip food
  • Panda's need at least 2 diffrent bamboo species in their range to avoid starvation

Interesting facts about Cicadas

  • Cicadas wings are transparent and can seem rainbow hued when held up to a light source
  • Cicadas when leave their burrow , sometimes make chimneys of mud over the exit
  • Each species of Cicadas has a different song

Interesting fun facts about Camel

  • Camels have the ability to completely shut their notstrils during sandstorms
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants which other animals can't eat
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes
